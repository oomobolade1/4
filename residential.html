Residential Living

Diverse housing options for all stages of life

Our Residential Offerings

Diverse Housing Options

Naawi-Oodena is planned as a walkable and sustainable community with a wide range of housing types to suit different lifestyles and stages of life. Whether you're starting a family, downsizing, or seeking community-focused living, there's a home here for you.

Low-Density Housing

This housing type provides a quieter, more suburban feel with homes that include private yards and generous space. These are ideal for families or individuals seeking a peaceful residential setting with more privacy and access to green areas.

Medium-Density Housing

Medium-density housing offers a balance between space and convenience. These buildings are typically low-rise and designed to foster a sense of community. They are located near shops and public spaces, providing easy access to daily essentials and gathering areas.

Affordable Housing

Our affordable housing options aim to create an inclusive and accessible neighborhood. These residences are built with thoughtful design, including features for accessibility and are part of mixed-income communities that promote diversity and mutual support.

Community Features

Green Spaces

Naawi-Oodena is enriched with beautiful green spaces, including parks, trails, and natural landscapes. These areas are designed to encourage outdoor activities, relaxation, and a strong connection to the land.

Walkable Design

The community is designed to be walkable, with homes, shops, schools, and public amenities all within a 15-minute walk. This approach supports healthy living and reduces dependence on vehicles.
